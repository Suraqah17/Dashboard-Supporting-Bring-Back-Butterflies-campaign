About Us

Who we are

ExploreiT Research is a start-up working towards youth empowerment and environmental activism. We aim to guide motivated, young students to expand their skill sets, achieve their academic goals and work towards creating a positive environmental impact. Our mission is to proactively engage and educate the youth in sustainable development and unite them for the fight against climate change.

ExploreiT was founded by Mr. Shivam Singh, the National Youth Coordinator for The Climate Reality Project India and an alumnus of XLRI Jamshedpur along with students of Symbiosis Centre for Management, Pune. The team consists of energetic and motivated youngsters who take up projects related to environmental activities in collaboration with various government institutes, Municipal Corporations, National NGOs, and corporate entities.

We have conducted multiple activities involving an active engagement of our countries youth, through numerous initiatives ranging from productivity workshops to cleanup treks.
Through our journey, we have partnered with several prestigious organizations and government identities such as the Pune Municipal Corporation, IBM, Climate Reality Project India etc., assisting and collaborating with them over several projects and events.
Our main objective is to unite the youth towards the achievement of carbon neutrality in India alongside upskilling them with the most relevant skills to scuplt them into strong, passionate and thoughtful leaders of our future.
